Our Classes

Charms

An absolutely essential class for any witch or wizard. taught by our own Professor Flitwick, you will master the 'swish and flick' in no time!

Potions

Some may not appreciate the subtle art of potion-making, but we believe it is absolutely necessary. Professor Snape teaches the exact art and science of potions.

Transfiguration

Some of the most complex and dangerous magic taught at Hogwarts! Professor McGonagall will teach you the art of changing forms.

Herbology

Learn about the many strange and unusual plants in our world and how to take care of them and use them with Professor Sprout!

Defence Against Dark Arts

One of the most popular classes at Hogwarts! Here, you will learn how to defend yourself against curses, hexes, jinxes and more. teacher TBD.

Flying

Take to the skies and learn the basics of broomology with Madam Hooch! Could you be Hogwarts' next quidditch star?

Our Houses

Gryffindor

Courage. Bravery. Daring. Nerve.

You might belong in Gryffindor, Where dwell the brave at heart, Their daring, nerve, and chivalry Set Gryffindors apart

Slytherin

Ambition. Cunning. Resourcefulness. Pride.

Or perhaps in Slytherin, You'll make your real friends, Those cunning folk use any means, To achieve their ends.

Ravenclaw

Wisdom. Intelligence. Creativity. Wit.

Or yet in wise old Ravenclaw, If you've a ready mind, Where those of wit and learning, Will always find their kind.

Hufflepuff

Fairness. Loyalty. Patience. Just.

You might belong in Hufflepuff, Where they are just and loyal, Those patient Hufflepuffs are true, And unafraid of toil.!
